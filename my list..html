Plant Name Plant Description
Monstera Deliciosa Known as the "Swiss Cheese Plant," this tropical beauty features large, glossy leaves with natural holes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and enjoys occasional watering.
Fiddle Leaf Fig (Ficus Lyrata) A striking plant with large, violin-shaped leaves. It prefers bright, indirect sunlight and consistent watering but can be finicky about changes in environment.
Pilea (Pilea Peperomioides) Often called the "Chinese Money Plant," it has round, coin-shaped leaves and is easy to propagate. It enjoys bright, indirect light and occasional watering.
Golden Pothos (Epipremnum Aureum) A hardy, trailing plant with heart-shaped green leaves streaked with yellow. It thrives in various lighting conditions and requires minimal care, making it a great beginner plant.
Peace Lily (Spathiphyllum) Recognizable for its elegant white blooms and lush green foliage, this plant thrives in low to medium light and helps purify the air. It prefers consistently moist soil.
